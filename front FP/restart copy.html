restaurant About the Restaurant

Abou El Sid is more than just a restaurant; it's a journey back in time to the 1940s Cairo. With its authentic decoration, copper tables, and warm lighting, it offers a nostalgic atmosphere. We specialize in traditional Egyptian cuisine prepared with age-old recipes, from our famous Stuffed Pigeon to the Royal Molokhia.
