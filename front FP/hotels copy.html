info About the Hotel

Step into a world of Victorian elegance and Egyptian charm. This historic British colonial-era hotel, built in 1899 by Thomas Cook, sits proudly on the banks of the Nile. It has hosted royalty and dignitaries, offering an unparalleled blend of history and luxury. Enjoy the infinity pool overlooking the Nile, the world-class spa, and fine dining at the famous 1902 Restaurant.
